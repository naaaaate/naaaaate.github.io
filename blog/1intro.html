My Very First Blog Post

Jul 14th 2015

(*** Taps the mic... Clears throat .... ***)

HI!

This is my very first blog post so I wanted to kick things off by introducing myself and a little about this blog.

I am originally from Louisiana (side note: we don't all dress/act/talk like the people you see on "Swamp People") where I've spent the past 7 years in an IT support position.After working as a support technician for numerous years, I wanted to venture out a little and begin learning how to code. I didnt know where to begin or what to create, all I knew was that I wanted to learn how to create something using a programming language! I've always had the desire to learn but for fear of failure was afraid to step out and begin the process.

Anyways, fast forward to early February 2015, where I got accepted to embark on this amazing journey called: Dev Bootcamp. Dev Bootcamp is a 19 week coding bootcamp aimed at transforming new programmers into jr web developers or as we like to call it: "World Class Beginners".

Now that I've finshed the program, I am currently searching for a role as a Jr. Developer while learning new technology everyday! I'm excited about coding and wanted to start blogging about the new things I'm learning.. hopefully in a way that any newbie who is in the same position I was in, can understand.

Hope you Enjoy the blog! and feel free to contact me with any questions or comments via twitter! (@n8leedbc)

-Nate
